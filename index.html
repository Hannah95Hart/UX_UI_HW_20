National Park Redesign

The current National Park Service website is dated and lacks a pleasing aesthetic and an easy-to-use flow.

Through user testing, ideation and development, we are going to create a design for the site that is more appealing, user friendly, and current.

Timeline: 5 weeks Scope: UX research, user testing, and surveys. Responsive web design, low to high fidelity redesign of the webpage, and a clickable prototype. Tools: Figma, Miro, Zoom, Google Surveys, Google Suite.
Research and Definition
To begin this process, we wanted to gather all the information we could, so we could take the best next steps to using RWD to rebuild this site.x
